# Atrial Fibrillation Template

## Monitoring Priorities
- Document palpitations, dizziness, fatigue, and exertional intolerance.
- Track heart rate trends and symptomatic episodes with time/duration.

## Medication Priorities
- Confirm anticoagulant adherence daily when prescribed.
- Reinforce rate/rhythm control medication consistency.

## Escalation Triggers
- Syncope, chest pain, severe shortness of breath, or neurologic symptoms.
- Sustained rapid heart rate with lightheadedness or hypotension symptoms.
- Any interruption of anticoagulation without physician guidance.
