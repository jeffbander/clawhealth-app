# Heart Failure Template

## Monitoring Priorities
- Daily morning weight before breakfast.
- Blood pressure and heart rate tracking at least 5 days/week.
- Watch for edema, orthopnea, and exertional dyspnea.

## Medication Priorities
- Confirm adherence to GDMT (ARNI/ACE/ARB, beta blocker, MRA, SGLT2 if prescribed).
- Reinforce diuretic timing and missed-dose instructions per physician plan.

## Escalation Triggers
- Weight gain >2 lbs in 24h or >5 lbs in 1 week.
- New shortness of breath at rest, chest pressure, or confusion.
- Progressive lower-extremity swelling despite diuretic adherence.
